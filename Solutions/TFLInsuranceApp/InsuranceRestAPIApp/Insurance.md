Common responsibilities in an insurance company

Sales Department: Sell new policies and assist customers.
Accounts Department: Process premium payments, refunds, and financial records.
Claims Department: Review, approve, reject, and settle claims.
Renewal Department: Handle policy renewals and send reminders.
Customer Support: Respond to customer queries and update information.
Compliance/Audit: Verify KYC, ensure regulatory compliance, and conduct audits.
